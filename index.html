About us

Built to move business forward.

Super Speed Delivery Service was established in 2021 in Dubai by Mr. Hamza Jeridi. What started as a focused delivery operation has grown into a fast-scaling logistics network operating across the UAE and Saudi Arabia. Today, we manage a fleet of over 700 riders and operate across 6 major delivery platforms, ensuring speed, reliability, and consistent service for our partners and customers.

01

Multi-Modal Delivery Fleet

We operate a versatile fleet of motorcycles, cars, bicycles, and walkers to match every route and delivery need.

02

Regional Coverage

Spanning Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and Ajman in the UAE, plus Riyadh in Saudi Arabia.

03

Strong Platform Partnerships

Working with leading delivery platforms including Talabat, Noon, HungerStation, Keeta, and Marsool.

04

Scalable Rider Network

More than 700 riders distributed across multiple cities, with the flexibility to scale capacity based on demand.

05

Reliable Last-Mile Execution

Focused on speed, accuracy, and fewer delays through responsive operations and optimized rider allocation.
