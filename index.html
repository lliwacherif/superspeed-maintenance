About us

Powering the Future of Delivery

Super Speed Delivery is a leading last-mile logistics and rider outsourcing company based in Dubai, helping businesses scale with speed, reliability, and operational excellence. Since 2021, we have partnered with delivery platforms and logistics companies to provide professional riders, fleet solutions, and end-to-end operational support across the UAE and Saudi Arabia.

Driven by innovation and backed by a highly trained workforce, we are committed to delivering consistent performance, seamless operations, and long-term value for every partner we serve.

We don't just deliver orders. We power the businesses behind them.
